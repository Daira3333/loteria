GORRO

"Pequeño y elegante, el gorrito siempre adorna con estilo."

"The Gorrito card in Lotería symbolizes charm and tradition. Depicted as a small, often decorative hat, it represents the finishing touch to a well-put-together outfit. In Mexican culture, the gorrito is an accessory worn on festive occasions, adding a touch of elegance and style. This card reflects the importance of attention to detail and the joy of celebrating with a sense of fashion."
